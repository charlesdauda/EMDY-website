ABOUT

From the English word ‘ethereal’ — meaning heaven — Etherean Mission is a Heavenly Mission anchored on earth.

ABOUT E.M DYNAMIC YOUTH

Etherean Mission Dynamic Youth is the vibrant youth wing of Etherean Mission (Heavenly Mission). We empower young people to live with purpose, creativity and spiritual awareness — awakening the divine potential in every young soul.

Our vision is that of a unified world in which everybody is a catalyst activating and promoting the innate good in one another.

MISSION
  • Provide you with the Life Tools to unleash your innate God potentials for inner peace and world peace.
  • Unite the Family of Humanity.
  • Restore cultural Self-dignity and respect especially for African people.
OUR OBJECTIVES
  • To bring spiritual enlightenment and empowerment to humankind.
  • To promote Oneness of Life.
  • To restore the Mystical Traditions of Africa.
